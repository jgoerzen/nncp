Integration with multimedia streaming

Many video and audio streams could be downloaded using youtube-dl program. Look how many of them are supported, including Dailymotion, Vimeo and YouTube.

When you multimedia becomes an ordinary file, you can transfer it easily. 

$ youtube-dl \
    --exec 'nncp-file {} remote.node:' \
    'https://www.youtube.com/watch?list=PLd2Cw8x5CytxPAEBwzilrhQUHt_UN10FJ'
